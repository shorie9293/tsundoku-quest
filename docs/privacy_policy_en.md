# Tsundoku Quest — Privacy Policy

**Established**: May 16, 2026
**Last Updated**: May 16, 2026

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Tsundoku Quest ("the App") collects the following information:

1. **Account Information**: Email address, password (securely managed by Supabase Auth)
2. **Library Information**: ISBN, title, author name, cover image, reading status (tsundoku / reading / completed), reading time
3. **Usage Statistics**: Reading timer usage duration, in-app activity logs (anonymized)

## 2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely for:

1. Account creation and authentication
2. Providing bookshelf management and reading records
3. Personalizing the app experience (reading level display, title awards, etc.)
4. Anonymized usage analysis to improve service quality

## 3. Data Storage and Management

- Personal information is encrypted and securely stored via Supabase
- Passwords are hashed — not accessible even to the app developer
- Data is managed in accordance with Japanese law

## 4. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share personal information with third parties except:

1. With user consent
2. When required by law
3. When necessary to protect life, health, or property and obtaining consent is difficult

## 5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

1. View and correct registered information
2. Delete their account and all associated data
3. Request data export

## 6. Advertising & Tracking

The App does NOT use:

- Behavioral targeting ads (AdMob, etc.)
- Third-party analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, etc.)
- Cross-app tracking
